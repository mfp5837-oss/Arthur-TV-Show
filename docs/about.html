What our Project is About

Our project is about an Erie, PA native who created the popular TV show Arthur on PBS. We analyzed seasons 1-4 for the first 3 episodes. When we worked on each episode, we reviewed the storyline and the most frequently used words in the episodes. Then we created word clouds that showed which words were used in it, and this was indicated by the size of the words.
